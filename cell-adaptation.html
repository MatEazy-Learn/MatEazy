Understanding Cell Adaptation

Cells are remarkable in their ability to adapt to changes in their environment. This process, called cellular adaptation, allows cells to maintain homeostasis despite various stressors.

Key Concept

Adaptations can be physiological (normal responses) or pathological (due to disease).

Important

When adaptations are exceeded, cell injury or death can occur.

Major Types of Cellular Adaptation

Atrophy

Decrease in cell size due to reduced workload, loss of innervation, or diminished blood supply.
